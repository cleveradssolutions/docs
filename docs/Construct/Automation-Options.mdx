---
title: Automation options
description: CAS offer automation features to simplify working with ads.
---

## Consent Flow Auto show if required
To collect user consent for personal data, use the built-in **Consent Flow**. It shows a consent form automatically during startup.

## Banner Ad Auto load
Automatically loads ads retry loading the ad if an error occurs during the loading process.

<Warning>
Unlike other formats, for automatic loading to work you need to trigger banner ad loading at least once and specify the required size.
</Warning>

## Banner Ad Auto Refresh
Interval (in seconds) for refreshing banner ads. Once elapsed, a new ad automatically loads and displays. Set 0 to disable auto refresh ad.

## MREC Ad Auto load
Automatically loads ads on startup and retry loading the ad if an error occurs during the loading process.

## MREC Ad Auto Refresh
Interval (in seconds) for refreshing banner ads. Once elapsed, a new ad automatically loads and displays. Set 0 to disable auto refresh ad.

## AppOpen Ad Auto load
Automatically loads ads on startup and after they are shown and retries on load errors.

## AppOpen Ad Auto Show
Automatically show ads when the user resumes a previously launched app. This automation does not mean the ad will be shown immediately upon loading.

## Interstitial Ad Auto load
Automatically loads ads on startup and after they are shown and retries on load errors.

## Interstitial Ad Auto Show
Automatically show ads when the user resumes a previously launched app. This automation does not mean the ad will be shown immediately upon loading.

## Interstitials Ad Min Interval
Minimum time (in seconds) required between interstitial ad displays.

## Rewarded Ad Auto load
Automatically loads ads on startup and after they are shown and retries on load errors.

## Collect Location
Collect from the device the latitude and longitude coordinated truncated to the hundredths decimal place.

Collect only if your application already has the relevant end-user permissions.
- Does not collect if the target audience is children.
- Disabled by default.

## (Optional) Trial ad-free interval
Defines the time interval, in seconds, starting from the moment of the initial app installation, during which users can use the application without ads being displayed while still retaining access to the Rewarded Ads format. Within this interval, users enjoy privileged access to the application's features without intrusive advertisements.
