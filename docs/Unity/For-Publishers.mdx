This guide helps publishers prepare a project for working with CAS.AI. By the end you will have analytics and event data flowing out of your app, and the attribution setup needed to run a test purchase.
 
Complete both parts together with your CAS.AI manager, who will provide the keys and credentials referenced in the steps.
