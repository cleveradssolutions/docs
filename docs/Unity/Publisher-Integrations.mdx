<Success>
You don't need to complete any additional integrations if your app is not part of the CAS.AI Mobile Game Publisher program.
</Success>

This guide helps publishers prepare a project for working with CAS.AI Mobile Game Publisher. By the end you will have analytics and event data flowing out of your app, and the attribution setup needed to run a test purchase.
 
Complete both parts together with your CAS.AI manager, who will provide the keys and credentials referenced in the steps.
